## Subject scope
Accessible graduate introduction. Covers Normal-Gamma conjugate family, posterior predictive checks, Gibbs sampling, hierarchical models, and Bayesian model checking.

## Coverage map (which task types this book grounds)
- NORMAL_GAMMA: Normal-Gamma conjugate update (Ch.5)
- PPC: posterior predictive checks (Ch.6)
- GIBBS: Gibbs sampling fundamentals (Ch.6)
- HIERARCHICAL: hierarchical normal models (Ch.8)
